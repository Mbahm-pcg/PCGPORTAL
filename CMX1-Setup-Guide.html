Five Steps to Get Set Up & Ready

1
OPEN THE APP

Enter the Portal Name

The first time you open CMX1, it will ask you for a portal name. This tells the app which company's system to connect to.

In the portal field, type: dunkinbaskin

The second field will auto-fill to .compliancemetrix.com. Leave that alone.

Tap Continue when you're done.

Tip: All lowercase, one word, no spaces. You only do this once — the app remembers it after setup.
2
SIGN IN

Use Your Inspire Brands / The Center Credentials

The Inspire Brands login screen will appear (red logo at the top).

Use the same username and password you use for The Center — these are your Inspire Brands SSO credentials. If you can get into The Center, you can get into CMX1.

Check the "Keep me signed in" box so you don't have to log in every day. Tap Next to continue through the login flow.

Tip: Forgot your password? Reset it on The Center first (thecenter.dunkinbrands.com). The same new password will work in CMX1.
3
INITIAL SYNC

Let the App Download Its Content

After login, CMX1 will sync your activities, store list, and audit templates to your device. This usually takes 30 seconds to 2 minutes depending on your connection.

You'll see a progress bar showing how many MB have downloaded. Stay on Wi-Fi for the fastest sync. Do not close the app while this is running.

Once it finishes, you'll be taken to your home screen automatically.

Important: You must sync again every time before you walk into a store — this is what pulls the latest activities assigned to you. See Step 5 for how to manually sync.
4
YOU'RE IN

Your Home Screen — Ready to Work

You'll land on the Dunkin' / BR home dashboard. From here you can:

• Select Activity — pick the audit or window activity you're working on (e.g. "DD Window Preparation")

• Calendar — see scheduled activities by date across the month

• Quick Links — jump to My Restaurants, Activities, Franchisee BI, or The Center

Your name appears in the top-right corner. That's how you know you're logged in correctly.

You're ready. Tap into a scheduled activity on the calendar, or use "Select Activity" to start a new one.
5
SYNC BEFORE EVERY STORE VISIT

How to Manually Sync Your Data

Before you walk into any store to complete an activity, you must run a manual sync. This pulls down the latest activities, updates, and templates assigned to you. If you skip this, you may be working from stale data.

The sync path:

⚙
Tap the Gear Icon
Top-right corner of the app
→
🔄
Find "Datasync"
Category in settings menu
→
✅
"Synchronize Data Now"
Tap to start the sync

Wait for the sync to finish before closing the app or heading into the store. The same progress screen from Step 3 will appear while it runs.

Rule of thumb: No sync, no visit. If the app hasn't been synced in the last few hours before you walk in, sync first — every time, every store, no exceptions.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

  • Portal name rejected? Make sure you typed dunkinbaskin — no space, no capital letters, no "dunkin baskin" or "dunkinBR".
  • Login says password is wrong? Try logging into The Center on a browser first. If it works there, reset and try again in CMX1.
  • Stuck on syncing? Check your Wi-Fi or cell signal. If stuck over 5 minutes, tap Cancel, close the app, and reopen.
  • Can't see your store or activity? Do a full sync (pull down from the top of the home screen). If still missing, you may not be assigned — contact your DM or Mike directly.
  • App crashes on launch? Force-close, reopen. If it persists, uninstall and reinstall — you will only need to redo portal + login, not the full setup.
