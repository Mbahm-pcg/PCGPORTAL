Deal Pipeline

A dedicated command center for every real estate deal — leased and purchased — from the moment we start chasing a site through LOI, due diligence, and lease execution or closing. Everything that happens before we break ground, in one place.

Phase 11.1 · Pre-Construction Real Estate
Separate from the Construction tracker
Restricted & server-secured access
📍

What it does

The Deal Pipeline owns a site for its entire pre-construction life. You work deals by stage, capture every term, store the paper trail with full version history, and never miss a critical date. The moment a deal hits Ready for Construction, it hands off to the development tracker and clears out of this view — so the pipeline only ever shows what's still in play.

Critical dates & reminders

Every deal carries its critical dates — LOI expiration, DD expiration, earnest money goes hard, financing contingency, closing, lease execution, delivery, rent commencement, construction commencement, and option / renewal notice deadlines. Each date has configurable advance warnings, not day-of alarms.

180 days120 days90 days60 days30 days

Example lead-times for an option/renewal notice — you set the tiers per date. Missing one of these is how you lose a location or get locked into rent you didn't want, so the runway is built in.

How to use it

Open the Deal Pipeline tab. In the sidebar under Team & Sites (visible only to authorized users).
Add a deal. Hit + New Deal, give it a name, type (lease / purchase), brand, and state. It lands in Sourcing.
Work it down the board. Drag through stages as it progresses; open a card to fill in lease or purchase terms and log activity notes.
Attach the paper. In the deal's Documents section, upload the LOI, lease / PSA, title, Phase I… re-upload to add a new version.
Set the critical dates. Add each date with its warning tiers (defaults pre-fill). Click Add to Calendar for an .ics, and let the daily digest watch your back.
Hand off. When the site is ready to build, move it to Ready for Construction — it leaves the pipeline for the development tracker.
🛡️
Built for confidential terms.

This module holds executed agreements and financial terms, so it's protected differently from the rest of the portal: a real, server-verified login issues a signed token, and every data and document request is authorized on the server — view vs. edit by role. The tab is restricted, and the documents sit in a private store the public endpoints can't reach. Access is managed by People Capital Group leadership.
